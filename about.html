About

Hello!

My name is Sanghyun Lee!

I am a current master’s student pursuing a degree in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan. My North Star is to leverage the information processing power of artificial intelligence and create new programs that can connect people at deeper interpersonal levels. Especially, driven by my industrial design background, I believe optimizing the use of the connected products can make a great impact on our human relationship. Therefore, in my spare time, I am having fun designing my own connected product using Raspberry Pi to better understand the limits and potentials of the Internet of Things.

In the past, I have interned as a UX researcher at IBM Design and worked as a UX designer at TmaxOS. Currently, I’m working as a UX designer at DocNetwork through the InfoLab program in a four-person team to improve the health record system for the account managers at DocNetwork.

Outside of the design world, I am a rock climber who loves to explore and craves the rush of adrenaline at the top. Rock climbing is like solving a puzzle through body movements and different techniques. I especially love to hang out and collaborate with my friends to figure out how to reach the top.
